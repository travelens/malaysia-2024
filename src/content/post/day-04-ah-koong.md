Not completely satisfied, we headed to a second yong tau foo restaurant recommended by a friend.

It was still raining when we arrived, so once again we were lucky to find parking right outside the restaurant. That was really fortunate, because all the available parking spots filled up soon after we arrived and we saw several cars circling the restaurant looking for parking.

There was a much larger variety of yong tau foo at this restaurant, but we were still quite full from the previous restaurant (Ah Choon) so we ordered only the ones we really liked. The quality of yong tau foo was much better here than the previous place so we were glad we had the chance to experience the food here.
