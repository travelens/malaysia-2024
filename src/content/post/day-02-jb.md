## KLIA2

We then checked out of the hotel and headed back to KLIA2 to check in.

![Chris](../../assets/2024-09-02/11_KLIA2_Chris.jpg)

![Lyn](../../assets/2024-09-02/12_KLIA2_Lyn.jpg)

We took AirAsia flight AK 6042 from Kuala Lumpur to Sepang. The flight took about 50 minutes and we landed just after 1pm. After arrival at Sepang Airport, we hired a car at AVIS. The AVIS person at the counter was so impressed I can speak Malay that we were upgraded to a brand new car!

![AVIS](../../assets/2024-09-02/21_AVIS.jpeg)

## Madame Kwan at JB Premium Outlet

After that, we headed to Johor Premium Outlet for some shopping and lunch. We tried Madame Kwan. I had the otak otak fried rice and Lyn had the Ipoh Hor Fun.

![Lyn](../../assets/2024-09-02/32_Madame_Kwan_Lyn.jpg)
