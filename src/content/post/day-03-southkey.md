## Aborted road trip to Kelapa Sawit

We originally intended to visit Kelapa Sawit, a town famous for street art. However, along the way, it started raining very heavily. I had the windscreen wipers on maximum, and it was barely clearing the raindrops.

A tree branch fell across the highay and blocked traffic for over half an hour. Fortunately, in the spirit of Malaysian "gotong royong", a few men got out of their cars and cleared the branch away from the road.

After that, we decided to abandon the road trip and head towards Midvalley Southkey, a high end mall located on the eastern side of Johor Bahru. We last visited this mall in 2019 when it was newly opened. I was disappointed that a few shops have closed down - mainly flagship stores like Tokyu Hands and a Japanese stationery shop.
