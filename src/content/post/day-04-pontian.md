On our last day in Johor Bahru, we checked out of the hotel and decided to drive to the town of Pontian. The skies looked dark and the weather was predicting imminent rain, but we decided to chance it and hope the weather remains clear for as long as we dared to hope for.

The way to Pontian was a single lane each way narrow road that passes through palm tree plantations. It reminded me a lot of the roads when I was a child, and it seemed I was transported in time.

When we reached Pontian, we weren't sure where to go and I decided to head to the beach area. This turned out to be a charmind promenade with a night market and food stalls on the southern end, some parks in the middle and a mosque, stadium and rest house on the northern end.

We were very lucky that it did not rain until we started heading back in the car.
